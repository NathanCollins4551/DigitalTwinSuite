Using Convai AI

The MakerSpace Digital Twin features an advanced AI Assistant powered by Convai. You can interact with the environment and get information through natural language.

T
Voice Interaction

Hold T to speak. Release to send your voice command.

↵
Text Interaction

Press Enter to open the chat box, type your prompt, and press Enter again.

Example Prompts

  • "Tell me about the MakerSpace"
  • "What model printers are found in the MakerSpace"
  • "How many printers are in the makerspace?"
  • "How do I start a print in the MakerSpace?"

AI Actions

The AI assistant can perform physical actions within the digital twin environment:

Note: Machines are referred to for actions by their station number (e.g., A4 or B5). This resolves the confusion between a station location (A1) and a machine model (Bambu A1).

● "Show me..."

The AI will walk to the specified machine and highlight it for you.

Prompt: "Show me printer B3"
● "Display Dashboard..."

Opens the detailed data view for the specified machine.

Prompt: "Display dashboard for printer at station D9"
● "Highlight..."

Highlights the specified machine to find easily in the digital twin.

Prompt: "Highlight printer A2"
● "Move to..."

The NPC will move to the specified machine.

Prompt: "Move to printer C6"
