Using Object Tracking

Object tracking in the MakerSpace is visualized directly within the Digital Twin. The environment is split into two distinct zones to monitor and manage inventory placement effectively.

Automated Inventory Placement

When a camera identifies inventory (such as filament) entering a zone, the Digital Twin automatically updates to show that item on the central inventory table.

Dynamic NPC Interaction

The central table is split into two halves, each representing a tracking zone. When inventory is moved from one zone to another, the AI NPC will physically pick up the item and move it to the correct side of the table in real-time.
